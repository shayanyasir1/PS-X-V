FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions commonly received by PlanetScope and VineList.

What is PlanetScope?

PlanetScope is a deep-space exploration organization dedicated to cataloguing newly discovered worlds and recovering lost cosmic transmissions.

What is VineList?

VineList is a vinyl-based audio company that transforms recovered planetary frequencies into collectible analog discs.

What was the purpose of this partnership?

PlanetScope partnered with VineList to preserve recovered deep-space transmissions through collectible vinyl discs. The goal was to transform mysterious planetary frequencies into physical media that could be studied, archived, and experienced by the public.

How do you benefit from working together?

PlanetScope provides exploration data and recovered signals while VineList converts those signals into unique audio experiences. Together they combine scientific discovery with analog sound preservation.

How do you make the patterns on the discs and what are they made out of?

The patterns are generated using real frequency scans collected from deep-space transmissions. Each disc is pressed using layered synthetic vinyl compounds mixed with reflective materials to create planet-inspired visual designs.

How does selling unique vinyl discs promote PlanetScope?

Every vinyl release spreads awareness of PlanetScope discoveries and exploration missions. Collectors gain access to hidden transmissions, classified logs, and encoded planetary data connected to each release.

Are the transmissions real?

Some recordings are authentic recovered signals from exploration drones while others remain under investigation.

What happened to Drone PS-01?

Drone PS-01 stopped transmitting shortly after descending into the atmosphere of Vireon-3. Only fragmented audio was recovered.

Can civilians visit the planets?

Most catalogued worlds are restricted due to environmental hazards and unexplained anomalies.

Why are some planets classified?

Several worlds contain unstable environments, unknown lifeforms, or transmissions considered dangerous to public release.

How are the vinyl discs made?

Recovered frequencies are converted into audio waveforms and pressed into custom-designed vinyl patterns inspired by each planet.
